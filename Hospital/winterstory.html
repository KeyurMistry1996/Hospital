Winter clinics

The winter season brings with it many unpleasant elements that can affect your health. Winter clinics allow users with minor health problems such as the flu, gastroenteritis, and other seasonal infections who do not have access to a family physician to obtain a medical consultation. Before visiting a winter clinic, have you thought of calling Info-Santé at 8-1-1? Calling Info-Santé 8-1-1 often helps avoid going to a medical clinic or emergency room. Make the right choice!
