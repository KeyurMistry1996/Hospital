About US

Careers

If you are interested in joining an internationally recognized team of professionals devoted to making a positive difference in the lives of children and families, then General Hospitals of — Canada is the place for you. Our brand-new, state-of-the-art facility is conveniently located adjacent to the McGill University Health Center (MUHC) at the Glen site.

General Hospitals of — Canada offers career opportunities for a variety of positions, clinical and nonclinical, as well as educational and research opportunities. The work environment is mission driven, high quality and provides great satisfaction to all professionals through the unique health care setting.

Health Careers – Find the industry’s best and brightest!

We are committed to delivering value to the health system and to our members, and that includes helping members with their unique recruitment challenges. That's why the General Hospital launched Health Careers, a valuable service that supports our member organizations and the health care community as a whole, recruit health care professionals and managers, medical practitioners and other support workers.

Recruting members can post their organization's career opportunities on Health Careers to attract the industry's best and brightest talent. We will also be sharing feature jobs through General Hospital latest hub for news and information - Health System News - so your posting can reach an even broader audience!

If you're a health care job seeker, then you'll want to visit Health Careers to search for the perfect job and sign up for job alerts!
